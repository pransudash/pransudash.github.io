Chit Chaat

Chit Chaat is one of ISA's more casual events of the year. It is a simple event created to bring together the South Asian community and beyond through he exciting street foods of India, while enjoying bollywood music, several themed activities and great company. Students come and enjoy the various snacks we provide including pani puri to samosas including their traditional mixes with classical Indian sauces (chutneys). Not only does one get a "taste" of India literally, but also one gets a good sense of the communal bonding ISA is able to foster through the games and even the final karaoke session we put on at the end. Anyone who comes leaves with seeing new faces, a good bite to eat and a great sense of the India's rich culture. Keep a look out on our Facebook page and website for further details.

Movie Night

Come enjoy the great Bollywood Cinema, snacks and refreshments with us. Movie night is an event hosted multiple times through the semester to watch different Bollywood Movies. It is a great opportunity for Indians on campus to be reminded of the music, drama and beauty of Indian Cinema. The movies showcased will have subtitles so feel free to bring any and everyone you know! Keep a look out on our Facebook page and website for further details.
